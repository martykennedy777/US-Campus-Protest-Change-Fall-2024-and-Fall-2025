Full replication files and code that extract and refine CCC protest data and create visualization of campus protest change. Included files:
1. The R Code to produce the dataset and visualization (SociusReplicationFile.R)
2. Input files necessary for the R code
   a. US Department of Education College Scorecard data on college and university characteristics (Replication_IPEDSData.csv)
   b. A list of locations confirmed to be located on college campuses, manually determined through checking CCC college protest data (Replicationkey_correctprotests.xlsx)
   c. A list of locations confirmed not to be located on college campuses, manually determined through checking CCC college protest data (Replicationkey_deleteprotests.xlsx)
   d. Information on state political party and control breakdown for 2025, according to the National Conference of State Legislatures (Replicationpolitical_party_breakdown.xlsx)
3. Output files produced from the R code
   a. The visualization submitted (ReplicationVisual.png)
   b. Descriptive statistics of my analytical sample (DescriptiveStatistics.csv)
   c. The final college campus protest dataset (n = 1812) (Replication_ProtestDataset.csv)
   d. Data on the percent change of protests comparing Fall 2024 to Fall 2025 based on individual institutional characteristics of colleges used in the visualization (Replication_ProtestVariableChange.csv)
   e. Data on the percent change of protests comparing Fall 2024 to Fall 2025 based on groupings of institutional characteristics used in the visualization (Replication_ProtestGroupVariablesChange.csv)
